About the artist

Alka Pradhan, professionally a doctor, art being her passion ! Born and brought up in Baroda (The princely state of Gaekwads, before independence) and now called Vadodara, the cultural capital of Gujarat. She did her graduation in medicine from Baroda Medical College . Later on she perused her studies in Public health at Ahmedabad for her MD.

Though she did not undergo any formal or professional training in art, she was very much influenced by works of art and artistic background of her family, from her childhood.

She is married and settled in Ahmedabad, where she served in Government of India 's health services. Currently, she is associated with social organizations, working for public health.
